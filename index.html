The Artist

Every portrait tells a story only you know

Based in Evesham, Gordon has been painting animal portraits for over a decade. He works predominantly in oils and detailed pencil, drawing out the warmth, personality, and quiet dignity that make each pet irreplaceable.

Every commission begins with a conversation. Gordon takes time to understand your pet - their quirks, their favourite spots, the way the light catches their eyes - before a single brushstroke is made.

Choosing to have a portrait painted is a big deal because it’s about celebrating a best friend. I take that responsibility to heart. My studio is a space of patience and slow, careful work, where I layer every stroke until the portrait finally looks back at me with the same warmth your pet does. It’s my way of making sure that the quiet dignity and joy they bring into our lives stays with us forever.
